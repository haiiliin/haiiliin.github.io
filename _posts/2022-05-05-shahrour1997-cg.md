# Abstract

This paper includes a presentation of cyclic interface tests performed with smooth and rough surfaces on loose and dense silica sand under either a constant value of the normal stress or a zero normal displacement. These tests are then used for the elaboration of an elastoplastic constitutive relation based on the bounding surface with two families of surfaces. The constitutive relation is checked over cyclic tests performed with rough interface on both loose and dense sands.
